Guidelines for Reporting

  • Be Clear and Concise:

    Write your problem clearly in simple language. Avoid jargon and be direct.

  • Provide All Details:

    Include relevant details like application numbers, dates, locations, and names of officials if known.

  • Attach Proof:

    If you have any supporting documents like receipts, applications, or photos, please attach them.

  • State Desired Outcome:

    Clearly mention what resolution you are seeking from the agency.

  • Keep Records:

    After submission, save the grievance ID or reference number for future tracking.
